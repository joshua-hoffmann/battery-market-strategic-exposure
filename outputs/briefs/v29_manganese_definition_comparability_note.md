# V2.9 Manganese Definition Comparability Note

## Purpose

This note resolves the V2.5 high-priority manganese source gap at the claim-boundary level.

Manganese remains analytically useful, but it is not currently comparable in the same way as lithium after V2.8.

## Inputs

- data/processed/v24_downstream_concentration_comparison.csv
- data/curated/v25_source_gap_enrichment_plan.csv

## Output

- data/curated/v29_manganese_definition_comparability_register.csv

## Comparability result

The manganese downstream value should remain classified as a definition-limited proxy.

The relevant IEA evidence concerns battery-grade or high-purity manganese sulphate. That product-specific denominator is not automatically equivalent to general manganese processing/refining.

Therefore, the project may use manganese to illustrate a high-concentration downstream battery-material proxy, but it should not describe manganese as having a fully validated general processing/refining concentration gap.

## Accepted now

- manganese can remain in the V2.4 downstream comparison;
- the downstream value can remain as proxy evidence;
- the source gap is clarified as a product-definition and denominator issue;
- future manganese enrichment should focus on denominator-aligned evidence.

## Not accepted now

- no validated general manganese processing/refining concentration metric;
- no bottleneck proof;
- no vulnerability ranking;
- no disruption probability;
- no investment signal;
- no policy recommendation;
- no geopolitical ranking;
- no claim that the manganese proxy is methodologically equivalent to the lithium IEA 2025 refining metric.

## Claim boundary

V2.9 supports a bounded descriptive proxy classification. It does not create a new manganese model, forecast, investment conclusion, policy conclusion, geopolitical ranking, or validated risk model.

## Next work option

The next productive step is to create a compact cross-mineral traceability summary for V2.4 to V2.9, separating source-traceable values from proxy-limited and context-only values.
