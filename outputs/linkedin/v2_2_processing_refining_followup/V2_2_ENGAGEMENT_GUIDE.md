# V2.2 Engagement Guide

Prepared: 2026-06-23

## Goal

Respond to comments in a way that preserves the analytical boundary of the project.

## Safe Response Framing

Use:

- This is a concentration snapshot.
- The analysis compares mine-production concentration with processing/refining concentration.
- The goal is to show where concentration remains after extraction.
- Higher concentration does not automatically mean higher risk.
- This is not an investment recommendation or forecast.

## Avoid

- Calling the output a risk score
- Calling it an investment signal
- Making geopolitical claims beyond the data
- Claiming supply disruption probability
- Claiming pricing impact
- Claiming company-level exposure
- Claiming policy conclusions

## Useful Reply Template

Thanks — the key distinction is that this is not a full supply-chain risk model. It is a concentration snapshot comparing mine-production and processing/refining concentration. The point is to show that a mining-only view can miss downstream concentration bottlenecks.
